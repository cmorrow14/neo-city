What we stand for

We believe the world doesn’t need bigger cities — it needs closer ones. Places where daily needs are met on foot, where community happens naturally, and where growth doesn’t destroy what already works.

  • Human-scale streets and blocks
  • Mixed uses in every neighbourhood
  • Public spaces that belong to residents
  • Long-term stewardship over quick profit

Current focus

Developing the first pilot district in Zanzibar as a proof-of-concept. Everything we learn there will become the approved template for future districts.
