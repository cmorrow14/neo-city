Kisiwa Mpya is a walkable, culturally rooted prototype city — starting in Zanzibar, designed for locals, and ready to inspire the world.

About NeoCity Vision Studio

NeoCity Vision Studio helps governments and communities co-create future-ready cities — ones that protect cultural identity, ensure public control of land, and invite the world without selling it off. Our pilot project, Kisiwa Mpya, is a 1-square-mile prototype in Zanzibar that shows how tourism, equity, and walkability can thrive together. We build with intention, not speculation.

What Does “Kisiwa Mpya” Mean?

“Kisiwa Mpya” is Swahili for “New Island.” It reflects the spirit of renewal — not by erasing the past, but by building on it. This name honors Zanzibar’s island identity while offering a bold symbol for what a better future can look like when shaped locally.
