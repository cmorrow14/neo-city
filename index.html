About NeoCity

“This isn’t just architecture. It’s storytelling, strategy, identity, and global positioning.”

NeoCity Vision Studio works with governments, planners, and early backers to imagine and shape the next generation of cities. We specialize in transforming underdeveloped coastal or trade corridors into thriving, respected, future-ready destinations. Our approach blends urban design, economic strategy, and place branding into one cohesive blueprint — because building a city is about more than concrete and capital. It’s about meaning, momentum, and identity.

Zanzibar: A Global Destination in the Making

  • Over 900,000+ tourists per year
  • Direct access to East Africa’s shipping corridor
  • Local support and long-term development interest

Our flagship vision reimagines Zanzibar as a luxury port, coastal innovation district, and tourism capital. We envision a city that blends timeless island culture with cutting-edge infrastructure, designed for climate resilience, international commerce, and sustainable growth. This is our prototype for what’s possible elsewhere.
