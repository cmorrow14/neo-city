Kisiwa Mpya Overview

Kisiwa Mpya means "New Island." It's our 1-square-mile prototype in Zanzibar — built around clean infrastructure, tourism, and dignity. The land stays public. The people benefit. The city grows as needed, not faster.

Our Mission

We build cities that create jobs, housing, and clean infrastructure without selling off land or importing foreign sprawl. Everything is leased, not sold, so the government retains control and local people benefit.

Grounded Approach

No hype, no buzzwords. Just real cities for real futures, designed with and for local communities.

Shared Prosperity

Through our lease model, we ensure economic benefits flow to local communities and governments.

Sustainable Vision

Our 1-square-mile prototypes focus on walkability, tourism, sustainability, and cultural preservation.

The Model

A new approach to urban development that preserves local ownership while enabling growth.

How It Works

99-Year Ground Lease

NeoCity asks for a 99-year ground lease — not ownership. This ensures the land remains in local hands while allowing for long-term development.

Public Trust Co-Governance

We work through a public trust co-governed with locals, ensuring transparent decision-making and equitable benefit distribution.

Land is leased, never sold
